Before Asking for Help

To get the fastest resolution, please:

  1. Check the documentation - Your question may already be answered
  2. Search existing issues - Someone may have encountered the same problem
  3. Try troubleshooting - Follow the troubleshooting guide
  4. Gather information - Collect logs, version info, and steps to reproduce

Security Issues

Important: Do NOT report security vulnerabilities in public issues.

For security-related issues, please email: security@helixagent.ai

We will respond within 48 hours and work with you to address the issue.

Community Guidelines

  • Be respectful: Treat others with kindness and respect
  • Be helpful: Share knowledge and help others learn
  • Be patient: Everyone is learning, including maintainers
  • Stay on topic: Keep discussions relevant to HelixAgent
  • No spam: Don't advertise unrelated products or services
