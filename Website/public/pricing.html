Frequently Asked Questions

Is HelixAgent really free?

Yes! HelixAgent is open source under the MIT license. You can use it for free in any project, including commercial ones. The paid plans provide professional support services.

What LLM providers are supported?

HelixAgent supports 18+ providers including Claude, Gemini, DeepSeek, Qwen, OpenRouter, Mistral, Cerebras, Groq, and more. You only pay for the API usage from your chosen providers.

Do I need a paid plan to use AI Debate?

No, the AI Debate system is included in the free open source version. All features are available at no cost - paid plans only add support services.

Can I self-host HelixAgent?

Absolutely! HelixAgent is designed for self-hosting. You have complete control over your data and infrastructure. We provide Docker and Kubernetes deployment guides.

What kind of support is included?

Free tier includes community support via GitHub. Professional tier adds priority email support with 4-hour response. Enterprise includes dedicated support engineer and 1-hour SLA.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes, you can change plans at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, and downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle. No long-term contracts required.
