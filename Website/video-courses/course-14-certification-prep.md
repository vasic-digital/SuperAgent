## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How long is the exam valid?**
A: Certification is valid for 2 years.

**Q: Can I retake the exam?**
A: Yes, after a 2-week waiting period.

**Q: Is the exam proctored?**
A: Yes, online proctoring is used.

**Q: What resources can I use during the exam?**
A: The exam is closed-book for written portion. Documentation is available for practical.

**Q: How soon will I get results?**
A: Written exam results are immediate. Practical assessment within 5 business days.
